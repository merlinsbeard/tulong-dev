  • What is tulong.ph?
    • It is a place where people can outsource tasks/jobs to Kaakays.
  • What is a Kaakay
    • They are people who joined tulong.ph to do the Poster's tasks.
  • What is a Poster
    • They are the ones who posts the jobs/tasks.
  • Is there a payment for joining tulong.ph?
    • There is no payment. There is a computed service charge after a successfull completion of tasks/jobs.
  • Is the work temporary or permanent?
    • The task/job is on the need basis only. there is no commitment after the task/job is done.
    • If the poster asks the Kaakay to be full time, it is up to the Kaakay to accept the offer. Tulong.ph is not part of it anymore.
  • What is the requirements needed to join tulong.ph?
    • Kaakay
      1. Picture of NBI Clearance
      2. Picture of Brgy. Clearance
        3. note* Name, address, and picture must be clear and readable.
    • Poster
      1. Picture of NBI Clearance
      2. Picture of Brgy. Clearance
        3. note* Name, address, and picture must be clear and readable.
  • What kind of jobs/tasks can be posted?
    • Anything under the sun. From construction to virtual or housekeeping to event planning. Anything goes that is legal of course.
  • As a poster, how do I pay?
    • You pay by Cash on Delivery Once the Kaakay is done with the Job/Task.
  • How much is the service charge?
    • For every successfull task/job. The Poster will pay an amount of P50 to tulong.ph through the Kaakay.
